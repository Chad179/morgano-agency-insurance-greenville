Is it always cheaper to bundle home and auto in Greenville?

Bundling is usually cheaper but not always. Some carriers offer aggressive home discounts but uncompetitive auto rates, and vice versa. The math favors bundling when the same carrier prices both at or near the lowest available. When carriers are not competitive on both, splitting may save money. An independent agent quotes both bundled and split scenarios and recommends whichever saves more after all discount stacking.
